## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to explore and/or perform a **web application security assessment** (penetration test) and related services. In connection with this, each Party may disclose confidential information to the other. This Agreement governs how that information is protected.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party to the other, whether written, oral, electronic, or visual, including but not limited to:

- Application source code, architecture, credentials, API keys, and configuration
- Vulnerabilities, security findings, proof-of-concept exploits, and reports
- Customer data, business processes, and internal documentation
- Pricing, scoping discussions, and engagement terms
- Testing methodologies, tools, and report formats (Consultant's confidential information)

**Confidential Information does not include information that:**

1. Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party  
2. Was already known to the receiving Party before disclosure  
3. Is independently developed without use of the disclosing Party's information  
4. Is rightfully received from a third party without confidentiality obligations  
5. Must be disclosed by law or court order (with prompt notice to the other Party when legally permitted)

---

## 3. Obligations

Each Party agrees to:

1. Use Confidential Information **only** for the Purpose stated in Section 1  
2. Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without prior written consent  
3. Protect Confidential Information with at least the same care it uses for its own sensitive data  
4. Limit access to employees/contractors who need to know and are bound by similar obligations  
5. **Not disclose the engagement publicly** (including portfolio, blog, or social media) without the other Party's written approval, except as required by law

---

## 4. Security Findings

Vulnerability details, exploit steps, and penetration test reports are **Client Confidential Information**. Consultant will not publish, share, or discuss specific findings outside the engagement without Client's written permission.

Consultant may use **anonymized, aggregated learnings** for internal skill development only (no company names, no reproducible details) unless Client approves otherwise in writing.

---

## 5. Return & Destruction

Upon Client's written request or within **30 days** after engagement ends, Consultant will:

- Delete Client credentials, screenshots, and raw test data from personal systems  
- Retain only: signed contracts, invoices, and a redacted summary needed for tax/legal records  

Client will delete Consultant's confidential pricing/methodology materials if requested.

---

## 6. Term

This Agreement starts on the Effective Date and remains in force for **3 years** after the last disclosure of Confidential Information.

Obligations regarding trade secrets survive as long as the information qualifies as a trade secret under applicable law.

Neither Party makes warranties about the accuracy or completeness of disclosed information.

This Agreement does **not** authorize penetration testing. Testing requires a separate signed **Rules of Engagement** document.
